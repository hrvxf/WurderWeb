# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Wurder processes only the data needed to run a fair and reliable social assassin game experience.

## What we collect

- Account and profile details you provide.
- Gameplay events required to manage lobby, live, and ended game states.
- Join and handoff telemetry for reliability and fraud prevention.
- Support messages sent to hello@wurder.app.

## How we use data

- To operate core gameplay mechanics such as host controls, kill claim flow, and disputes.
- To keep game code handoff reliable from web to app.
- To prevent abuse and investigate fair-play disputes.
- To provide support and safety responses.

## Sharing and retention

- We do not sell personal data.
- Service providers process data only to operate Wurder.
- Data is retained for gameplay integrity, legal compliance, and support continuity.

## Your choices

- You can request account deletion.
- You can contact hello@wurder.app for privacy requests.

## Contact

For any privacy question, email hello@wurder.app.
